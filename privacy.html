Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you submit your email address on our website, we store it to send you information about Netara's portfolio audit service and product updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

When you use the Portfolio Leakage Calculator, the inputs you enter (portfolio allocation percentages and total amount) are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or log this data. No portfolio information leaves your device.

Cookies and analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns — pages visited, referral sources, and session duration. This data is anonymised and not linked to individual identities.

Third-party services

Our newsletter is managed through Beehiiv. By subscribing, you agree to Beehiiv's privacy policy in addition to ours. We use Google Fonts for typography — font files are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's privacy policy.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@netara.in.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@netara.in.
